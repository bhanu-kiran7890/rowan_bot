Degrees & Programs
A Leader in University Academics
With more than 100 bachelor's, 63 master's degree programs, 16 doctoral programs and three professional programs, students can pursue any number of professional callings. Our nationally-ranked academic programs are taught in small classes, by faculty who are engaged with their students, connected in their fields, and invested in student achievement. For those unsure of what degree to pursue, our Exploratory Studies Program provides supportive advising and resources designed to allow students to learn more about a variety of majors and careers, until they find the path that is right for them.
Undergraduate Programs
Our nationally-recognized undergraduate programs are led by faculty who who bring real-world expertise into the classroom, ensuring personalized attention and a strong focus on student success.
Graduate Programs
With more than 63 graduate programs to choose from, our students pursue scholarly opportunities and career development through rich and rewarding educational experiences.
Rowan-Virtua School of Osteopathic Medicine
As the state's only college of osteopathic medicine, we prepare future physicians and scientists who are committed to improving health across New Jersey and the globe.
Rowan-Virtua School of Translational Biomedical Engineering & Sciences
We offer graduate programs and promote multidisciplinary translational biomedical research.
Cooper Medical School of Rowan University
Our clinical faculty are part of the Cooper University Health Care system, with 800 physicians in over 75 specialties.
Rowan Online
Rowan Online offers flexible and convenient 100% online coursework, allowing students to balance their studies with personal and professional responsibilities.
